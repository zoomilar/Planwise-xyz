Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is an IRA?

An Individual Retirement Account (IRA) is a type of savings account designed for retirement, offering tax benefits and flexibility in investment choices.

❓ Can I invest in stocks with my IRA?

Yes, most IRAs allow you to invest in a wide range of assets, including stocks, bonds, ETFs, and mutual funds. However, some IRAs may have restrictions on certain investments or asset classes.

❓ Are IRAs taxable?

IRAs are generally tax-deferred, meaning you won't pay taxes on contributions until withdrawal. However, withdrawals from a traditional IRA may be subject to income tax and potentially penalties for early withdrawal.
